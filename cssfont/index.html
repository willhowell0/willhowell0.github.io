GEO M-E: THE THREE SHAPE TYPEFACE

This typeface is primarily composed of three geometric shapes; triangles, circles, and rectangles. I chose this approach because I like the typefaces simplicity and how the shapes overlap to compose an individual letter form. This design could be effective across several platforms such as posters, billboards, magazine spreads, ect. Because there are so many elements to each shape, it is best used in headers rather than body text.
