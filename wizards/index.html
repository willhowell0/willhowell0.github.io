From the Baltimore Bullets, to the Washington Zephyrs, to the Washington Wizards, DC’s NBA sports team has had a unique history of uniforms that changed in design and color throughout the years. Wes Unseld, Michael Jordan, Manute Bol, and Gilbert Arenas are some of the notable alumni who have had the privilege to play for our nation’s capital. Each player can mark a period in NBA history and a uniform. As you take a look at the uniform throughout the decades, you can see how the fit, style, brands, and colorways change over time.
